FAQ

Frequently asked questions

What's included in the "double warranty"?
We guarantee runtime of 2,000 engine hours OR 730 days — whichever comes first. STRUM generators carry our standard warranty; partner brands follow each manufacturer's terms.
How does the 180-day deferred payment work?
Buy a generator today — pay in 180 days. The program is available for corporate clients with a positive credit history, subject to approval by our commercial department.
Can I rent a generator for a single event?
Yes. Rental starts from 1 day. We deliver, commission and collect after the event. Units from 10 to 500 kVA are available.
How fast does the service team respond?
In Kyiv and the region — from 4 hours. Across Ukraine — within 24 hours (depending on the region). Service phone: +380 98 018 39 76.
What is a cogeneration unit and why would I need one?
Cogeneration is the simultaneous production of electricity and heat from a single fuel (usually gas). Efficiency reaches 90%, compared to 35% for a classic generator. Ideal for sites with a stable demand for heat.

Need a generator or consultation?

Leave a request — an engineer will get in touch within 15 minutes and help pick the optimal model for your site.
